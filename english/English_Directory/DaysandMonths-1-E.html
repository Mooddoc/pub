Months & Days Explained

  • Days of the Week: There are seven days in a week: Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday.
  • Months of the Year: There are twelve months in a year: January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, and December.
  • Weekdays: The days from Monday to Friday are called weekdays.
  • Weekend: Saturday and Sunday are considered the weekend.
